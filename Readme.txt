🎮 Gurugrah Learning Universe

Gurugrah Learning Universe is an educational adventure game designed to make learning fun, interactive, and rewarding. Created by Atul Jangir, this game blends knowledge with gameplay, encouraging players to explore multiple subjects while enjoying an engaging experience.

🚀 Game Overview

In Gurugrah Learning Universe, players navigate through dynamic environments, overcome obstacles, and collect coins to progress. Along the way, they encounter special question cards that challenge their knowledge and help reinforce learning.

🧠 Game Modes

The game offers multiple learning modes:

Math Mode – Solve numerical challenges and improve problem-solving skills
Science Mode – Explore concepts from the world of science
Coding Mode – Test logical thinking and programming basics
Mixed Mode – A combination of all subjects for a complete learning experience
🎯 Features
🪙 Coin Collection System – Earn coins while playing
🧱 Obstacles & Challenges – Avoid hurdles to keep progressing
🃏 Question Cards – Answer questions to gain rewards and boost knowledge
⚡ Power-Ups:
Speed Boost – Move faster through levels
Shield – Protect yourself from obstacles
More exciting power-ups to enhance gameplay
🎮 Objective

The goal is to survive, collect as many coins as possible, and correctly answer questions to maximize your score while learning along the way.

🌟 Vision

Gurugrah Learning Universe aims to make education enjoyable by combining gaming mechanics with learning, helping players grow their knowledge in a fun and interactive way..
